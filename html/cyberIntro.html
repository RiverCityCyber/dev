How To Get Started In Cyber Security

The world of cybersecurity is incredibly vast and ever changing. Getting started in such a torrent of information can be daunting, but I am here to help. Below you can find a list of resources I personally find useful.

It is incredibly important for anyone wishing to start down the cybersecurity path to understand the basics of computers and computer networking. All internet traffic abides by the same rules, and understanding those rules means you can understand how to exploit them. If you are unfamiliar with computer networking, I highly recommend following my steps below.

  1. Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)
  2. CompTIA Security+
  3. Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)
    4. \
  4. Certified Information Systems Security Personnel (CISSP)

Get Involved!

The cybersecurity community is incredibly welcoming and helpful. Online studying and endless hours of coding are a cornerstone of the field, but so is collaboration and teamwork. Chances are that if you land a job in cybersecurity, you will be working with a diverse team of experts.

One of the best ways to grow and discover new fields within computing is by attending conferences. I personally have enjoyed the following conferences:

  • CyberBay
  • BSides JAX
  • Try Hack Me
  • Cloudflare Docs
  • OverTheWire
  • Professor Messor
